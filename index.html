About Us

Welcome to Pavitra Sarees! We specialize in exclusive wedding and traditional sarees. Visit our shop at the heart of Mumbai for a stunning collection.

Pavitra Sarees is the proud vision of Shantilal Vyas, who laid the foundation of this elegant brand on 29th December 2018 in Dombivli, followed by the grand opening of our flagship store in Dadar on 5th September 2024. With over 30 years of hands-on experience in the saree business, Shantilal Vyas brought not only deep knowledge of fabric and tradition but also a heartfelt commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Over the years, Pavitra Sarees has grown into a trusted destination for premium wedding and traditional sarees, loved by more than 8000+ satisfied customers across Maharashtra and beyond. Every saree in our collection reflects generations of craftsmanship, a touch of cultural pride, and the timeless beauty that every woman deserves to wear.
